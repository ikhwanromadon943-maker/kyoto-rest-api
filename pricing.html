Frequently Asked Questions
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?
Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the end of your billing cycle.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise plans.
Is there a free trial for Pro?
Yes! New Pro subscribers get a 7-day free trial. No charges until the trial ends.
What happens if I exceed my rate limit?
You'll receive a 429 Too Many Requests response. Upgrade to a higher tier to increase your limit.
