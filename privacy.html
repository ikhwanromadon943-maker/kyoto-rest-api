Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

Kyoto API ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our API services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • • Request Logs: API endpoint accessed, timestamp, and response status code.
  • • Usage Data: Number of requests made, rate limit consumption.
  • • Uploaded Files: Temporarily stored for processing, auto-deleted within 24 hours.

2. How We Use Information

  • • To provide and maintain our API services
  • • To monitor usage and prevent abuse
  • • To improve service performance and reliability
  • • To enforce rate limiting and security measures

3. Data Retention

Request logs are retained for 30 days. Uploaded files are automatically deleted within 24 hours after processing. We do not store personal data beyond what is necessary for service operation.

4. Third-Party Services

Our API may interact with third-party services (e.g., Jikan API, Pollinations.ai, Open-Meteo) solely to fulfill your requests. We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Security

All API traffic is encrypted via HTTPS. We implement rate limiting to prevent abuse. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

6. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us via WhatsApp or Telegram channels listed on our homepage.
